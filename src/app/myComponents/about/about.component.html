About Me

Hi, my name is Sarita Nimwal and I am an Angular Frontend Developer based in Bombay, India. I have 4.6 years of experience building scalable, high-performance web applications with Angular, TypeScript, RxJS, and modern frontend architecture.

I currently work as a Software Engineer at Lynkit Solutions Pvt Ltd, where I develop modular Angular applications, reusable UI components, analytics dashboards, REST API integrations, and geospatial features using Google Maps, Mapbox, and GeoJSON. I hold a B.Tech in Computer Science from MDU Rohtak and a Diploma in Computer Science & Engineering from Government Polytechnic, Manesar.

My strengths include lazy loading, performance optimization, code-splitting, component-driven design, Agile collaboration, and delivering responsive user-centric interfaces. I enjoy solving complex frontend problems and building systems that are maintainable as they grow.
